Welcome To Crypto Gods Ecosystem

Crypto Gods is an NFT P2E strategy game on Ethereum. Play, trade, stake, and get rewarded in $SHEN.

about us

Crypto Gods Ecosystem is governed by an exclusive collection of Egyptian Gods and is ruled by another unique collection of Egyptian Pharaohs. Pharaohs claim power over strategic ancient Egyptian land pieces. NFTs combined from each collection create powerful territories.

As owners stake their NFTs in the Pyramid and advance in the game they are rewarded in $SHEN utility token. Crypto Gods NFT owners unlock exclusive perks and rewards throughout the ecosystem and gain exclusive access to VOTK.

FAQ's

Where can I find further information?

Further information can be found in our documents page and on our Discord server. Kindly follow this link in order to access all of the details about Crypto Gods ecosystem. https://docs.cryptogodsecosystem.com.

Which Blockchain?

The ecosystem resides on the Ethereum blockchain.

Which gods are featured in CG?

We have selected the most powerful Egyptian gods in history. Horus, Seth, Anubis, Bastet and Sobek.

When will the mint begin ?

The Whitelist mint for the Gods Collection is coming in April 2022. The Whitelist mint will last for 24 hours. The public mint will begin 72 hours after the whitelist mint and will last until the collection sells out.

What is the VOTK?

The Valley Of The Kings is our holders-only central communication hub where all releases, IRL events and updates are announced while chilling with the Gods. Each member is assigned a dedicated seat and a custom obelisk. It is controlled by 42 judges carefully selected from the community. VOTK is led by the father of all gods, RA.

More about the Metaverse

Crypto Gods Metaverse is a P2E game played by the Gods and Pharaohs. Our holders will have early access to the Metaverse game where each holder will be assigned a role based on the character owned from the NFT collections.

What is in the Pharaohs Collection ?

All Informations about the Pharaohs collection will be released after the Gods collection sells out.

What is the utility token used in the Crypto Gods?

The $SHEN utility token is at the core of the Crypto Gods ecosystem. $SHEN is passively earned daily through staked Gods.

What kind of Rewards can we expect?

Crypto Gods has an extensive rewarding program to the holders. Join our discord to know more about the perks and rewards.

Is CG devoting anything to Charity ?

As part of our global social responsibility Crypto Gods team will host a series of charitable donations as the ecosystem develops.
